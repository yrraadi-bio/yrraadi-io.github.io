AI Designed CRISPR Systems

CRISPR has the potential to cure many diseases, like cancer.

Today's computational biology technology is not built on a foundational understanding of the genomic domain. Hence, there is no tool that allows for the consistent creation of CRISPR systems that edit the optimal complementary DNA. Frequent off-target editing, unforeseen autoimmune responses and unpredictable repair mechanisms also make today's options unreliable.

We're building Large Genomic Models (LGMs) to generate CRISPR systems tailored to the individual and target DNA. This will enable rapid discovery of novel gene therapies.
