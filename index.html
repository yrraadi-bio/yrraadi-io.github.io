AI Designed CRISPR Systems

CRISPR has the potential to cure many diseases like cancer.

Today, these systems are unsafe because of off-target editing and unforeseen autoimmune responses. As a result, they have restricted targeting options.

We're building Large Genomic Models (LGMs) to generate sgRNAs and Cas proteins tailored to the individual and target DNA. This will enable rapid discovery of novel CRISPR systems.
