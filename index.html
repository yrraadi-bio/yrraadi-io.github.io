The Platform
01

Tissue Collection

Fresh surgical resections sourced directly from partner hospital networks, preserving full viability and the native tumor microenvironment.

02

Precision Sectioning

Vibratome-cut slices that preserve native tumor architecture and the intact cellular microenvironment.

03

Perturbation

AI-designed chemical and genetic perturbations applied to live tissue with matched controls, simulating interventions to maximize information gain per experiment.

04

Multimodal Readouts

High-throughput measurement across transcriptomics, proteomics, and spatial modalities for comprehensive tumor profiling.

05

AI Analysis

Foundation models trained on perturbation response data, mapping tumor vulnerability landscapes and designing the next round of experiments.
